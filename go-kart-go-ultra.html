Go Kart Go! Ultra! Game is about to begin, so start your motors! Make a great choice for your driver and race through Old Station, Central Park, Watermill Mine, and other locations. Play Go! Ultra! Ultra is an online game that you may win to earn the best bragging rights. The player is required to finish all nine tasks.

The player can choose between the One Player and Two Players modes before the race. Split Screen Multiplayer is basically Two Players mode.

The only difficulties are easy races. Old Station, Central Park, Mexican Village, Candy Mountains, Fireland, and other names are given to some of these difficulties.

The player can choose a character by clicking on a challenge. Rhino Bert, Albert Von Schwein, and Freddy Flamingo are the only three characters present at first.

It is the first title in the Go Kart Go! series to be offered for mobile devices. Although there is no proof, it may be the last entry in the Go Kart Go! series. Go Kart Go! TurboSplit !'s Screen Multiplayer, also known as the Two Players option, is back. It is not, however, accessible via a mobile device.

The player must use pick-ups to battle the opponents in such challenges. Additionally, there are some paths with modest boosts for the player, like turbo pads. The player's goal while racing is to gather as many stars as they can in order to unlock more characters.
